The full workflow

Four steps. Zero manual work.

01

Capture

Guest info, dates, payment, and tax details pulled directly from your PMS. Custom fields like environmental fee are supported out of the box.

02

Generate

The right document, automatically: a receipt for standard guests, a VAT invoice when requested. Taxes calculated correctly, every time.

03

Submit

XML files sent directly to mydata.aade.gr. You receive a MARK confirmation number from the tax authority—proof that everything went through.

04

Correct

Need to annul or amend? Issue credit notes linked to specific invoices. Same-day corrections and partial adjustments supported.

Compliance

The law is clear. Your process should be too.

MyData compliance is mandatory for all Greek businesses issuing receipts or invoices—B2C, B2B, and B2G. Over €1,000? An invoice is required by law. Seasonal environmental fee rules apply automatically.
